TARGET AUDIENCE


Men and mostly women in the middle class between the ages of 21-50 years old that are high school graduates and have completed some college at least, or attended a trade school. This type of audience generally wants to excel in life and has their own thought-out plan. They are moderately tech savvy enough to look up weather quickly and usually on their mobile device, as that is how they access their calendar and other necessities to accomplish daily tasks.



Persona 1:

Jacob is a 45-year old man who manages a roofing company. He attended a trade school for roofing and has been in the industry for 20 years. It's taken time but he finds his iphone useful now and it for contacting his management team to ensure their scheduled jobs are on track for completion. He likes to stay ahead of the competition any way he can. He wants to ensure a secure financial future for his family of 4. In the early mornings he accesses the weather from his iphone, while still in bed, to check on possible job delays or necessary reschedules due to bad weather.


Persona 2:

Charlotte is a 33-year old stay-at-home mother with an associate's degree in business. Her 6-year old girl recently started attending a school located near their home. Charlotte is very tech savvy and uses her android phone to help her keep her home life organized. she uses her phone as a planner, task tracker, and alarm clock. When home she mostly uses her tablet to look up recipes and home repairs. Each morning she wakes up and checks the weather to see how she needs to dress her girl for school and to see if she needs to drive her, instead of walking with her as she usually does.




-SCENARIOS-


How can this help the average employee?

As a daily driver you need to understand and be aware of weather conditions in case of extreme weather that could slow down traffic and make you late for work. You would also know how to dress outside and inside your office building so you can feel well and perform at your best.


Can this help me have a low-stress home life?

Parents drop off their children at the school bus stop or may choose to drive them if weather conditions are rough. They also would need to know how to dress their young kids.


Does my income depend on the weather?

A real estate agent shows his clients houses to buy around town and hosts open houses that he posts signs around the neighborhood to advertise. The weather can make or breaks his day with showings and open houses. He would want to know when weather conditions cause difficulty in travel so he can plan his days when visibility and travel are good.


How can this help my business thrive?

This weather site will allow people like Jacob, whose business depends on the weather conditions, to be well-informed and use weather information to avoid disaster situations thus bringining in more customers. He will also be able to stay well-organized with the employees on call. If a need arises where more bodies are needed at the office, or on the field, he can call in extra staff on days they are extra busy rescheduling jobs due to bad weather.


Improved grades in school?

A college student needs weather information to know if she needs to leave home early to be on time for class. She also needs to know how to dress properly, while making sure her books/laptop are properly covered for her walk through campus to her class. Having a succesful class depends on her arriving to class prepared and not distracted by possible extreme weather conditions damaging her school materials and clothes.


Reducing unnecessary costs.

Outdoor businesses, like landscaping companies, thrive on weather conditions. When there is a job scheduled that involves an outdoor installation, if they don't check the weather they could face having to re-do a lot of jobs just due to bad weather conditions and that will cost the company twice as much.
