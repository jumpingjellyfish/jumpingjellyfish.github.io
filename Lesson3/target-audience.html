TARGET AUDIENCE


Men and mostly women in the middle class between the ages of 21-50 years old that are high school graduates and have completed some college at least, or attended a trade school. This type of audience generally wants to excel in life and has their own thought-out plan. They are moderately tech savvy enough to look up weather quickly and usually on their mobile device, as that is how they access their calendar and other necessities for daily tasks.



Persona 1:

Jacob is a 45-year old man who manages a roofing company. He attended a trade school for roofing and has been in the industry for 20 years. He uses his iphone for contacting his management team to ensure their scheduled jobs are on track for completion. He likes to stay ahead of the competition any way he can. He accesses the weather on a daily basis to check on possible job delays or reschedules due to weather.

Persona 2:

Charlotte is a 33-year old stay-at-home mother with an associate's degree. Her 6-year old girl recently started attending a school located near their home. Charlotte is very tech savvy and uses her phone to help her keep her home life organized. she uses her phone as a planner, task tracker, and alarm clock. When home she mostly uses her tablet to look up recipes and home repairs. Each morning she wakes up and checks the weather to see how she needs to dress her girl for school and to see if she needs to drive her, instead of walking with her as she usually does.




SCENARIOS


City bus driver:

As a daily driver he needs to understand and be aware of weather conditions in case of extreme weather that could slow down traffic and routes. He would also know what type of clothing to wear. He has an average level of tech savyness and needs to see the detailed weather conditions to prepare for his job.


Stay-at-home mother:

She drops off her children at the bus stop or may choose to drive them if weather conditions are rough. She also would need to know how to dress her young kids.


Real estate agent:

He shows clients houses to buy around town and hosts open houses where he posts signs around the neighborhood to advertise it. He would want to know when weather conditions are optimal for an open house.


How can this help my business thrive?

He makes back-up plans accordingly like calling in extra staff on days they need to reschedule or cancel jobs due to bad weather.
