Provo City Center Temple

The Provo City Center temple was announced on October 1th, 2011. It is the fourth temple to be built from an existing building. The groundbreaking was performed on May 12th, 2012 by Elder Jeffrey R. Holland and was dedicated on March 20th, 2016 by Elder Dallin H. Oaks. It is the sixteenth temple built in Utah.

Boise Idaho Temple

The Boise Idaho temple is the 27th dedicated temple in operation. It was announced on March 31st, 1982. The groundbreaking and site dedication was performed on December 18th, 1982 by Mark E. Peterson. It was dedicated by President Gordon B. Hinckley on May 25th-30th, 1984 and rededicated by Thomas S. Monson on November 18th, 2012.

Lima Peru Temple

The Lima Peru temple is the first built in Peru. It was the third temple built in South America. It is the 38th dedicated temple in operation and was announced on April 1st, 1981. The groundbreaking and site dedication took place on September 11th, 1982 and was conducted by Elder Boyd K. Packer. It was dedicated on January 10th-12th, 1986 by President Gordon B. Hinckley

Las Vegas Nevada Temple

The Las Vegas Nevada temple is the first temple built in Nevada and was announced on April 7th, 1984. The groundbreaking and site dedication was performed on November 30th, 1985 by President Gordon B. Hinckley. It was dedicated December 16th-18th, 1989 by President Gordon B. Hinckley. It sits on 10.3 acres and totals 80,350 square feet.
