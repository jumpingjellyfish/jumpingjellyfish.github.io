Are There Real Fish in Fish Haven?

by Airy Pete

It sure sounds like we can expect to find plenty of fish in this town. It's actually a lot less populated by fish than it used to be. Similar to the California Gold Rush, there really was a Fish Haven Fish Rush. That's right, we had fishermen flocking to our little city to get the best catch possible. It truly felt like a haven of fish back in those days. The sunlight was perfect and nature was kind. A lot of the men slept in the woods and preferred to live in nature during their fish-hunting season.

Today you still find fish, but the water is less occupied these days.
