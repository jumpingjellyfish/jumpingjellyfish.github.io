We Built This City

by Annonymous

We certainly have seen a lot of growth. What was once a small, farm town with small businesses has blossomed into what is now our very own Preston. Has it changed all that much? The town has expanded but the hearts of the people remain the same; welcoming and kind. It's not easy to keep the small town feel and local associations when experiencing growth, but somehow Preston has managed. It continues to be a place of friendly communities, yearly traditions and the quiet life.You don't need to look far to find rodeo events or beauty in nature. With many community activities and nearby sights to see, it's no wonder this town is often referred to as the "best kept secret in the west!"
